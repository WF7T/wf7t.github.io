Was honored to be a part of the 2022 ARRL DX CW contest team at K3LR. Fantastic time, as always, and I am pleased with our result. It is amazing to be in the same room with contesting legends!

At home, I put a new 40M dipole up in the trees about 55 ft AGL. Seems to work pretty well! I have been hoping to get more active in the CWOps and NCCC weekly operating events. Maybe see you in one or two. 

Also procured a new 6M yagi, a 4 element loop-fed configuration by EAntenna. Hope to have this up soon!

I did manage to work an ATNO along with some band/mode adding to my DXCC totals. As well, in my never ending quest toward the WPX Award of Excellence, I managed to confirm TWO new African prefixes...only six more to go! 

In late news, I am loaned an amplifier by my friend Kirk K4RO. Uh oh...here we go!
