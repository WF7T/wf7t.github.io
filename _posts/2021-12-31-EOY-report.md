Still busy professionally, so I am falling behind on my radio hobby!  ARRL 10M was a bust, and I was exhausted by the time Stew Perry Top-band Distance Challenge came around so I gave only a superficial effort. Propogation for me just wasn't there, and these are Butt-in-chair (BIC) events. If I am NOT BIC, then I will NOT be around for the propogation. I was not BIC, so no victims here.

End of year operating highlights:

* Finally finished WAZ Mixed; VU2TMP Thampi was my hero on 15M CW!
* Four all-time-new-ones (ATNO), 70 add'l DXCC Challenge band slots, 8 new DXCC on 60M.
* Slowly whittling away at 12 and 17 WAS, with 8 and 2 new ones respectively. It would help if I actually had a "real" antenna for each.
* Still don't have AK or HI on 6M, but I DID manage to work EU for the first time on 6M! 45 Grids worked on 6M, finishing 6 shy for the VUCC 300 endorsement.
* Seventy-five new WPX prefixes confirmed, putting me at 2162 total. Still need 8 more in Africa to qualify for the WPX Award of Excellence.
* Limited exposure multi-transmitter contesting with K4RO and K3IE in 2021...really fun fellows!

This past year has been a slow one as to projects. I made a job change, so I did not make a lot of operating or antenna project time. My priority was health, family, profession this past year. Can I make time for radio projects in 2022? I hope so!

Wishing you all health and happiness in 2022. See you on the bands!
