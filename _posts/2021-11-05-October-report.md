CW SS will be a casual-ish multi-two effort at Kirk K4RO's place, activating the Tennessee Contest Group's call K4TCG. Casual-ish simply because me and Kirk can't really be casual about contesting, so let's just agree that it will be a time-limited operation. All Qs will be appreciated, so please contact us if you hear us. I will probably be active as my own call on as Sunday "fresh meat."

Had fun chasing the recent DXpeditions in October: J5HKT, S9OK, 3DA0RU, 7P8RU. I am happy to add three more prefixes to my WPX totals for Africa toward my WPX Award of Excellence and to add to my 60M country totals. Great DXpeditions, great operators. Many thanks to the teams! 

I am delayed in doing any additional antenna work. Big plans, poor time management. I started a new job so I have been focused upon that priority instead of radio. So many radio projects that need my attention (am I right?)
